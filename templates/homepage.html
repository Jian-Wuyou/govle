Bringing platforms together...

GoVLê was born out of a need to keep track of classes across different online learning platforms, brought about by the shift to remote learning during the pandemic.

With GoVLê, you get a bird's-eye view of your classes from both Google Classroom and UVLê, hence the name GoVLê.

...privately and securely

We know how annoying it is to have yet another password to generate or remember, so we made it easy to log in to GoVLê: just log in with your University of the Philippines Google account.

Your Google Classroom and UVLê credentials are never stored in our database, and your UVLê password is protected before transmission with 4,096-bit RSA encryption.

Open source,
powered by open-source

GoVLê is completely open-source, and it cannot exist without other amazing open-source technologies.

The website is deployed directly and instantly from our GitHub repository, so you can be sure that updates are fast and authentic.
